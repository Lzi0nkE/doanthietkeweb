DOTA PIT

After taking a short break, our boys were eager to compete again. The first tournament we joined was season 2 of the OGA Dota Pit. Unfortunately a hand injury prevented Kuroky from playing with the team. rmN- stepped up and played in his place, where he was the sleeper hit of the tournament!

In the absence of Kuro, Miracle- took the role of captain and led the team in the tournament. With his leadership and help from Kuroky, we didn’t lose a single series all the way to the Grand Finals.

After a stellar performance throughout the tournament we faced Alliance in the Grand Finals. It’s always a welcome challenge going up against them. With a great showing by both teams, our series went all the way to the fifth Game and we came out on top winning the series and the tournament, undefeated to the end!
