ABOUT THE PLAYER

THERE IS NOT MUCH WE CAN SAY ABOUT SALEHI “KUROKY” TAKHASOMI THAT ISN’T ALREADY KNOWN!

Kuro is one of the pillars of DotA, starting his career in 2007 back in the DotA 1 days. Over his long career, he has set a number of records. He was the first player to ever reach 1000 competitive wins and he’s the only player to play with every hero in Dota competitively. Kuroky is one of two players who have been to every TI, reaching the Grand Finals a total of three times and winning it all at TI 7.

With Kuroky’s leadership, the future looks bright for Team Nigma.
